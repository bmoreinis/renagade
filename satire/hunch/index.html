Kids Turn Into Hunch Backs

By London Lester

Kids all around the world are slouching around at home all day this news could potentially lead to a hunchback. According to concerned parents they notice their kid’s slouching around at home all day and wonder will they look like The Hunchback of Notre Dame? They will or they will not.

Meredith Hirt, parent leader explains,Slouching is very common in kids, and they don’t realize the harm it causes. I try telling my daughter and son to stop slouching all the time, but nothing seems to work. I don’t know what else to do. I tell them if you don’t stop, you're going to have a hunch back.

Yet, despite this creativity, working at an improper desk and chair setup can negatively affect posture and may even lead to a hunchback appearance, or what is often referred to as postural kyphosis. Here's what you need to know about this condition, including important tips on prevention.” Concerned parents formed a group to talk about ways to help improve set-ups in their homes to prevent that.

According to ScienceDaily, moms have been telling their children not to slouch forever, and for good reasons. “Poor posture not only makes a bad impression but can actually make you physically weaker.... adopting dominant versus submissive postures decreases your sensitivity to pain.”

After interviewing a mother who is extremely worried about her kid slouching, has noticed some side effects. Jennifer Lawrence noticed her daughter’s back starting to stick out of her clothes. She can’t fit any of the clothes that fit her before. Lawrence now has to buy her oversized clothes that will feel comfortable and fit. Lawrence is also noticing her head starting to hang weird, it gives her extreme headaches. Lawrence got emotional explaining how her child looked a lot like the hunch back of Notre Dame.
