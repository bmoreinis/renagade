The Renagade: Purposeful Satire

This is the Renaissance Renagade, a satirical publication by the students of Springfield Renaissance. Satire is fiction writing that mimics the style of a news story, exaggerating the truth to make us question our world. The stories in this publication are all made up, but they are making fun of real things we are dealing with in our world, which they link to. Please enjoy it, and also think about it!
